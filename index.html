Quick and Easy Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Egg Fried Rice is a quick, tasty, and satisfying dish made with rice, eggs, and vegetables. It's perfect for a fast weeknight meal or lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cooked Rice (preferably cold)
  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (carrot, peas, corn)
  • 2-3 tablespoons Soy Sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Oil
  • 1 small Onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Spring Onions for garnish

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onions till fragrant.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  3. Push vegetables to one side and scramble the eggs on the other side.
  4. Add cooked rice, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix everything well.
  5. Stir-fry for a few minutes until heated through.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
